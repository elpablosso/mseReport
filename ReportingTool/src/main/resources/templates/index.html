Welcome in Reporting Center

App scope

Main goal of this application is to store informations and details about projects and employees. Information are stored in the database. The main purpose of the application is to enable users to make daily reports. The information contained therein will be assigned to specific projects, and can be used to create summaries, and facilitate the analysis of the project in its individual sectors.
